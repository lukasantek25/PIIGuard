# PIIGuard Privacy Policy

PIIGuard does not collect, store, or transmit any user data.

All text processing happens locally in your browser. Your text is never sent to any server owned by PIIGuard.

The extension stores only your preferences (which entity types to detect) locally using Chrome's built-in storage API. This data never leaves your device.

We have no backend, no analytics, no tracking, and no data collection of any kind.
